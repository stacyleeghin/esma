How can I help the Animals?

Since the Egyptian revolution began in February 2011, ESMA has been feeding and treating the starving and sick horses in Nezlet Al-Samman, near the Giza pyramids, whose owners have been devastated by the drop in tourism. Between 300-500 horses receive enough food for a week to ten days at each feeding.

ESMA relies entirely on donations and contributions from supporters both in Egypt and abroad to keep going. Your time, resources, and expertise are vital to us and to the animals we serve.
